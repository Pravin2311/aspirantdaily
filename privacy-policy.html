We collect minimal data:

2. How We Use Your Data

3. Cookies

We use only essential cookies:

  • _ga, _gat, _gid (Google Analytics)
  • No third-party advertising or tracking cookies

4. Data Security

All data stored in your browser (localStorage) remains on your device and is never sent to our servers. We do not store any personal information on our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
